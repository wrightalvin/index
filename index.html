ALVIN WRIGHT FIRST NATIONS RECREATION CONSULTANT

​

Proposal to Plan & Implement a Preventative Maintenance Program for First Nations Recreation Facilities & Programs & Services

​

My name is Alvin Wright. I reside from my Nisga’a community of New Aiyansh. I come from the Eagle clan, from the house of L’aay, my Nisga’a name is Lugiloon, which means, “Three Persons in One Three Persons Together.”In August 2002 I enrolled at Langara College in Vancouver BC, in the Recreation Diploma Program .In April 2005 I graduated with a scholarship from Langara College. . June of that year I began my employment with then New Aiyansh Village Government. In August we invited Mr. Vince Kreiser, coordinator for Langara College Recreation Diploma Program to assist us to plan, develop, and implement a Preventative Maintenance Program for, New Aiyansh Recreation Centre. During the nine years, as Gitlaxtaamiks Village Governments Recreation Director I’ve gained the knowledge and experience to plan, develop and implement an effective PM program and Program & Services for First Nations Recreation Facilities.A PM Program is an essential component to have in place before planning, developing and implementing Programs & Services for your facility. An effective PM Program, will show positive results each year to your “Bottom Line”.
